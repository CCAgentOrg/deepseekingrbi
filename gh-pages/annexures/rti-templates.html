RTI Application Templates — DeepSeeking Accountability in RBI

Purpose: Three recommended RTI applications to obtain critical documents currently outside the public record. Each template is drafted for filing with the Central Public Information Officer (CPIO) of the relevant public authority under the Right to Information Act, 2005.

RTI Application 1: Procurement Byelaws & Single-Source Award Justification

To: CPIO, Institute for Development and Research in Banking Technology (IDRBT)

Address: Castle Hills, Road No. 1, Masab Tank, Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh 500057

Subject: RTI application for information regarding procurement byelaws and single-source contract award for the .bank.in domain registry portal

Body:

1. Please provide a certified copy of IDRBT's current Procurement Byelaws / Purchase Manual, including any amendments made since 1 January 2020.

2. Please provide copies of all Governing Council resolutions, committee minutes, or internal notes authorising the award of the .bank.in domain registry portal development and maintenance contract to IKCON Technologies (CIN: U72900AP2018PTC109917) without a competitive tender.

3. Please provide the following details regarding the .bank.in domain registry portal contract:

a. Date of award

b. Contract duration

c. Total contract value (including any renewal or escalation clauses)

d. Procurement method used

e. Justification for single-source / no-tender award

4. Please confirm whether IDRBT's byelaws or procurement policy were amended between 1 January 2023 and 30 June 2024 to increase the threshold for competitive bidding. If yes, provide a copy of the amendment resolution and the date of Governing Council approval.

Fee: ₹10 (postage stamps) enclosed.

Format: Certified photocopies / PDF copies on CD.

RTI Application 3: IDRBT Security Audit Records

To: CPIO, Institute for Development and Research in Banking Technology (IDRBT)

Address: Castle Hills, Road No. 1, Masab Tank, Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh 500057

Subject: RTI application regarding security audit of the .bank.in domain registry portal

Body:

c. Scope of audit

Fee: ₹10 (postage stamps) enclosed.

Format: Certified photocopies / PDF copies on CD.

Filing Notes

1. Fees: Each application requires ₹10 (postage stamps / Indian Postal Order) payable to the concerned authority.

2. Appeal route: First appeal to First Appellate Authority (IDRBT Director / designated officer) if no response or incomplete response within 30 days. Second appeal to Central Information Commission (CIC), New Delhi.

3. Timeline: Response due within 30 days (Section 7(1), RTI Act). 48 hours for life/liberty matters — not applicable here.

4. Exemptions to anticipate: Section 8(1)(d) (commercial confidence), Section 8(1)(e) (fiduciary relationship), Section 8(1)(j) (personal information). Grounds for rejection may apply to contract value and security audit details.

5. Recommended filing: By registered post with acknowledgment due (RPAD) for proof of delivery.

6. Follow-up: If rejected, file first appeal within 30 days of receipt of CPIO's decision.

7. CIC citation: If appeals fail, WP(C) before Delhi High Court under Article 226 for non-implementation of RTI Act.
