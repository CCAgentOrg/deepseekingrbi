> **Mission**: Systematically document, evidence, and publicise the governance failures in RBI's network of subsidiaries, controlled entities, and departments — through the lens of the `.bank.in` debacle, the IDRBT procurement bypass, the revolving door, and the systemic entity proliferation that enables all of it.

## Verified Data Points (Source-Graded)

All factual claims are graded per the system in `chapters/rbi_governance.md`:
- ✅ **Confirmed**: Multiple independent verifiable sources
- ⚠️ **High confidence**: Strong logic + partial verification (e.g., source + ZoomInfo)
- 🔴 **Allegation only**: Source claim, no public document yet
